Product Overview

CarePhrase Communication Academy is an AI-powered communication training platform designed for health and social care staff.

The platform helps learners develop the communication skills required to deliver safe, person-centred, and professional care through realistic workplace simulations, structured feedback, guided practice, and scenario-based learning.

The platform does not provide clinical advice, replace professional judgement, or generate official records. Its purpose is to improve communication competence, confidence, and decision-making in health and social care environments.

Vision

To become the leading communication skills training platform for health and social care professionals in the UK.

Mission

To help care workers communicate more clearly, confidently, safely, and professionally through practical, scenario-based learning.

Problem Statement

Many health and social care incidents are linked to communication failures rather than lack of technical knowledge.

Common challenges include:

Poor handovers
Incomplete escalation of concerns
Inconsistent safeguarding communication
Difficulty communicating with families
Lack of confidence during emergencies
Language barriers for international staff
Inexperienced staff struggling with professional communication
Limited opportunities for realistic communication practice

Existing training often focuses on compliance and theory rather than practical communication skills.
